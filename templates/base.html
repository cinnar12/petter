About Petter

Petter is a pet browsing app dedicated to helping you find the perfect companion. The app allows you to look through dogs and cats available for adoption in the Gainesville area and features adoptable pets from Gainesville Pet Rescue, Helping Hands Pet Rescue, Alachua County Humane Society. However, there are many more animals looking for their furever home. Below is a map of rescues and shelters where you can find other adoptable animals in the area.

Why Should You Adopt?

There are many reasons why you should adopt a dog or cat. Here are a few:

  1. You can save a life! Millions of healthy, adoptable animals are euthanized every year. If you adopt, you can make room for other animals to come in.
  2. It'll cost you less. Adopting a dog or cat can be a lot cheaper than purchasing through breeders and pet stores, and other upfront costs are sometimes already included in the adoption fee.
  3. And most importantly, you'll get a great pet! You can bring home a perfectly healthy dog or cat whose age and experience can match your needs.
